## Translations

Community translators who brought BATorrent to their language:

- 🇺🇦 **Ukrainian** — [@dkindratyuk-web](https://github.com/dkindratyuk-web)
- 🇹🇷 **Turkish** — [@azizaktas](https://github.com/azizaktas)

Want to add or improve a translation? Open a PR with `translations/<lang>.json`
— copy `en.json` and translate the values, keeping every key (the app checks
key parity across all languages).
